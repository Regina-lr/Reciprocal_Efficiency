# Reciprocal_Efficiency

Codes and behavioral data for

R. Liao, X. Li, X. Liu, Y. Nan, X. Zhou, and X. Gao, “Efficiency Recalibrates Social‐Emotional Trade‐Offs Behind Partner Choice in Direct Reciprocity through Intention‐Specific Neural Bases.” Adv. Sci. (2025): e16509. https://doi.org/10.1002/advs.202516509

This paper has been accepted by Advanced Science. Title: Efficiency Recalibrates Social‐Emotional Trade‐Offs Behind Partner Choice in Direct Reciprocity through Intention‐Specific Neural Bases.

The data of fMRI study, including the unthresholded first-level contrast images of each participant, are avaliable on OSF: https://osf.io/hxp7g/

The raw fMRI data are protected and are not available due to data privacy laws.
